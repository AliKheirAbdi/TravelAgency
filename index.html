Most Popular Destination

Maasai Mara

The Masai Mara is always an amazing safari destination, but at its best during

the wildebeest migration, which occurs here between July and October each year.

The park is surrounded by concession areas and tribal lands of the Maasai people.

These are unfenced from the Mara and there can be as much wildlife roaming outside

the park as inside so staying in these private concessions can often be just as rewarding.
